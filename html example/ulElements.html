    Origin
    Nadella[9][10][11] was born into a Telugu speaking family[11] in Anantapur, Andhra Pradesh, India. His father, Bukkapuram Nadella Yugandher, was a civil servant of the Indian Administrative Service.[12][13] Nadella attended the Hyderabad Public School, Begumpet[14] before attaining a bachelor's degree in electrical engineering[15] from the Manipal Institute of Technology in 1988 (then affiliated with Mangalore University), Manipal, Karnataka.[16][17][18][19][20]
